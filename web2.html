Information About Engineering

Engineering is the practical application of science, mathematics, and creativity to design, build, and optimize structures, machines, systems, and processes. It is a problem-solving discipline that converts scientific discoveries into functional technologies, spanning from infrastructure and electronics to energy systems. Key branches include civil, mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering.
